Who are we?

We are a group of computer specialists who operate in Burton and South Derbyshire College.We are able to fix a wide variety of devices to a good standard using our experience with hardware and software while maintaining a professional standard of productivity.


What is our objective?

Our primary objective is to help reduce the amount of E-waste and we aim to achieve this through recycling electronic devices when possible, which will allow for a more sustainable future, the image shown below is a stock image demonstrating the inside of a computer.
